About the Zeus Browser

Zeus is a free and open source Webbrowser based on WebKit. The Browser in an early development phase,
but it's mostly ready to use.

Why should I use Zeus instead of Firefox, Chrome and Opera?

Because it's fast safe and doesn't steals your personal data.
You can convince you self by use some independent Browser tests.
